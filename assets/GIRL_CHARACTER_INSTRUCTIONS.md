## Animation Requirements

### Character Design
- **Cute cartoon girl** (child-friendly, simple design)
- **Indian/Gujarati appearance** (appropriate cultural representation)
- **Warm, friendly colors** (pastels, soft tones)
- **Age:** Appears 6-8 years old

### Animation Actions
1. **Girl appears** (fade in or simple entrance)
2. **Smiles warmly** (friendly, welcoming expression)
3. **Waves hello** (gentle hand wave gesture)
4. **Duration:** 3-4 seconds total
5. **Loop:** No (plays once)

### Style Guidelines
- ✅ Simple, clean design (not overly detailed)
- ✅ Bright, cheerful colors
- ✅ Smooth, gentle movements
- ✅ Calm and non-distracting
- ❌ No complex/busy backgrounds
- ❌ No text or speech bubbles
- ❌ No sudden/jerky movements

## How to Obtain This Asset

### Option 1: LottieFiles.com
1. Go to [lottiefiles.com](https://lottiefiles.com)
2. Search for: "girl waving" or "child greeting" or "cartoon girl hello"
3. Filter by:
   - Free animations
   - Simple style
   - 3-4 second duration
4. Download the `.json` file
5. Rename to `Cute Tiger.json`
6. Place in `/assets/` folder

### Option 2: Custom Creation
Use tools like:
- **Adobe After Effects** with Bodymovin plugin
- **Figma** with Lottie export plugins
- **Haiku Animator**

### Option 3: Commission
Hire a Lottie animator on platforms like:
- Fiverr
- Upwork
- LottieFiles marketplace
